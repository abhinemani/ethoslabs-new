Government technology is often critized: failed, massive projects that get more headlines than users. But the blame can't be placed on the public servants at work, desparately trying to get the job done with broken tools and outdated policies. Indeed, it's often those people who are the most keen on changing the system -- and that's who we want to work with. Based on experience working with hundreds of govtech leaders, EthosLabs is here to help them (read: you) find a better, modern way.
